Mental Health Disorders Around the World

Mental health involves aspects including psychological, emotional, and social well-being. It plays a large role in how we feel, think, and behave. 2.2 million Australians have reported having a mental health condition (Australian Institute of Health and Welfare, 2022).

This visualisation looks at the prevalence of various mental health disorders worldwide, then focuses more specifically to statistics within Australia. This includes data on mental health strategies and services.

Prevalence of Mental Health Disorders Around the World

Prevalence of Anxiety Disorders Around the World

Individuals suffer from anxiety disorders worldwide. The countries with the most prevalent cases of people with anxiety disorder are Portugal, New Zealand, Brazil, Ireland and Switzerland.

As you scroll across the years, it is evident that the prevalence of mental health disorders worldwide generally increases as time goes on.

Prevalence of Schizophrenic Disorders Worldwide

Different regions show different trends in the prevalence of Schizophrenic Disorders. The World Health Organisation (WHO) region that has the highest prevalence of schizophrenia disorders is the Region of the Americas, where in 2005 there was a prevalence of 0.37%

Comorbidities between disorders

Mental health disorders are often comorbid, meaning that there is usually a two or more mental health conditins that individuals suffer from simultaneously.

There is a positive relationship between the prevalence of alcohol use and drug use.

Mental Health in Australia

Prevalence of Mental Disorders in Australia

Does the likelihood of developing a mental health disorder change as we age?

The ABS defines any long-term mental disorder as any mental disorder that has been occurring for at least 12 months. Evidently, prevalence of mental disorders decrease as age increases. It can also be seen that females have a higher prevalence of mental health disorders than males.

Strategies Used for Mental Health

Individuals cope with their mental health in a variety of ways. In Australia, out of those with mental health disorders, the method used with the highest prevalence is through increasing the level of exercise/physical activity undertaken, followed by practising positive thinking.

Cutting alcohol or drugs is the least prevalent method of dealing with mental health issues.

Click the bar to highlight a particular strategy used for mental health.

Types of Mental Health Services Used

There are a variety of mental health services that can be accessed by Australians when they are looking to seek professional help.

Out of the Australian population, general practitioners are the most common type of mental health service used, followed by psychologists. The least popular mental health service used is allied health professionals.
