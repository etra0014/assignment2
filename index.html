Mental Health Around the World
Mental health involves aspects including psychological, emotional, and social well-being. It plays a large role in how we feel, think, and behave. 2.2 million Australians have reported having a mental health condition, according to the 2022 Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) data.

Mental Disorders in Australia

Does prevance of mental disorders change as we age?

The ABS defines any long-term mental disorder as any mental disorder that has been occurring for at least 12 months. Evidently, prevalence of mental disorders decrease as age increases. It can also be seen that Females have a higher prevalence of mental health disorders than men.
